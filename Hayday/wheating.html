"Wheating is a farming strategy in HayDay."

1: Why Wheat?

Wheat is a quick crop, ready in only 2 minutes. It's also a great source of expansion materials like land, barn, and silo upgrades.

2: Faster Harvesting

Harvesting can be a bit slow. You can try harvesting with 2 fingers instead of one.

3: Avoiding Mistakes

Before starting harvesting, ensure your barn and silo have enough space. If they're close to being full, you won't receive expansion rewards from wheat harvesting. Consider selling excess items like eggs, coal, ores, etc, to make room for expansion materials.

3: Your Marketplace

Keep a good amount of slots in your Marketplace, save up and buy them! That way you get rid of the unecessary amounts of wheat quicker.
